Bridging Theory and Practice: Unraveling My Internship Experience

ixp Summer Internship at SAP Labs, Bangalore

15th May 2023 - 14th July 2023

During my summer internship at SAP Labs Bangalore, I had the incredible opportunity to be a part of the esteemed Team BTP Core, specifically working within the dynamic sub-team APIHub.
Throughout this enriching experience, I was deeply involved in a cutting-edge project focused on the automation of the release process for SAP's highly acclaimed product, Business Accelerator Hub.
My main responsibility was to streamline and automate the release process using two powerful tools, Jenkins and GitHub. Leveraging these technologies, I designed and implemented an efficient and reliable release automation system that significantly reduced manual intervention and streamlined the entire process.
This not only resulted in improved productivity but also ensured faster and more reliable releases of the Business Accelerator Hub, ultimately benefiting SAP's customers and stakeholders.
Apart from the technical aspects, the internship also reinforced the importance of effective communication, teamwork, and project management. I was encouraged to voice my ideas and opinions, and my contributions were valued and respected by the team.
This experience not only strengthened my interpersonal skills but also gave me a sense of belonging and fulfillment in contributing to a larger collective goal.
